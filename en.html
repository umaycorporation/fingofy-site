Fingofy - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 02, 2025

The app works offline/online together for payment plans, budget, goals and analytics.

Overview

Fingofy helps users manage their finances. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Collected

Account and Identity

  • Email (Firebase Authentication)
  • Name/surname, language preference

App Data

  • Payment plans, transactions, budgets, goals and related fields
  • Settings (theme, currency, notification preferences)

Device and Usage

  • Notification token (for push notifications)
  • Basic usage and diagnostic information

Data Sources and Storage

  • Local: Temporary storage on device for offline work and re-synchronization
  • Cloud: Account and app data on Firebase (Authentication, Firestore)

Data Processing Purposes

  • Authentication and account management
  • Data synchronization and backup
  • Payment reminders (optional)
  • Analytics and performance improvement
  • Security and legal obligations

Legal Bases

  • Performance of contract
  • Legitimate interest (security, debugging)
  • Explicit consent (e.g., notifications)

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication / Firestore (Google LLC)
  • Google Play Services

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing may occur only for legal obligations or security reasons.

Security

  • HTTPS/TLS transmission security
  • Firebase Security Rules and access control
  • Secure storage approaches on device

Retention Periods

  • Account data: Until account is deleted
  • App data: Until user deletes or purpose is no longer valid

User Rights

  • Access, rectification, deletion
  • Restriction/objection to processing
  • Withdrawal of consent (notification preferences)

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If detected, it will be deleted.

Changes

The policy may be updated. Important changes may be announced in-app.
